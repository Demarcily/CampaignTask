Desc

Ever since Covid-19 came there has been an absurd amount of disinformation and conspiracy theories spreading around, theories that are extremely dangerous because of their tendency to target minorities, ignore scientific evidence and twist words to fit their narrative.

Because of this disinformation and conspiracy theories which can deal great harm to a large amount of people we decided to try and do something about it, what we are planning on doing is to actively debunk any kind of conspiracy theory related to Covid-19 that pops up while also correcting any form of disinformation that might occur.

We will do our best to spread facts about Covid-19 around social media such as YouTube, Twitter, Reddit, etc. And we hope that you will aid us in our goal by further spreading our messages or using the sources we provide to battle disinformation.

About us

We are a group of people who are sick of seeing disinformation wherever we go on the internet and we want to do something about it and we want to get rid of this sickening disinformation or at the very least decrease it so less people will be tricked and we will do so by sharing evidence and facts in our own website and posts in others and we will provide what sources we use.
